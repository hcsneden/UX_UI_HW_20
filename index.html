London

A brief guide to an amazing city

Things To Do

Check out Street Art:

Shoreditch and Brick Lane specifically is known for its street art. Along this road there are many vintage shops to check out.

See a Show:

London has some of the best theater in the world with something for everyone. I saw Wicked in 2015 and was blown away.

Explore New Areas:

Explore up and coming areas. Areas like Dalston and Hackney are great places to check out and see what’s new. Most of the best parts of the city are the ones where you have space to explore and create your own narrative.

Food

Street Food:

Street food at markets offer some of the best food I’ve ever had. It is easy and fast and typically pretty cheap.

Indian Food:

London has some of the best Indian food, no matter what neighborhood you're in.

Pubs:

While there isn't wold class food at most pubs, going to a pub in London is a rite of passage.

About Me

I had the pleasure of studying abroad in London in 2015, and over the course of 4 months, I learned more about myself than I could ever imagine. As cliche as that last sentence was, I really did have an amazing experience exploring London. I hope you like my recommendations and are able to experience everything this great city has to offer.
